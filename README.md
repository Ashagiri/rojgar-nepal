## 🇳🇵 Rojgar-nepal | Open-Source Job Portal

 **An open-source job portal dedicated to connecting skilled professionals with top employers across Nepal.**

Rojgar-Nepal is an open-source web-based job portal developed to connect talented professionals with employers across Nepal. The platform aims to simplify the recruitment process by providing a centralized space where job seekers can discover career opportunities and organizations can efficiently find and hire qualified candidates.



In today's competitive job market, finding the right opportunity or the right talent can be challenging. Rojgar-Nepal addresses this problem by offering a modern, secure, and user-friendly platform that streamlines the entire hiring process. Job seekers can search and filter vacancies based on skills, location, and industry, while employers can post jobs, manage applications, and identify suitable candidates through a dedicated dashboard.

Built using **Python**, **Django**, and **PostgreSQL**, the platform emphasizes scalability, security, and performance. Django's robust architecture enables the development of reliable backend services, while PostgreSQL provides efficient and dependable data management. The system is designed to support future enhancements and increasing numbers of users without compromising performance.

Rojgar-Nepal is developed with the vision of contributing to Nepal's growing digital ecosystem and promoting employment opportunities through technology. As an open-source project, it encourages collaboration from developers, designers, and contributors who are passionate about building innovative solutions for the recruitment sector. Community contributions are welcomed to help improve features, optimize performance, and expand the platform's capabilities.

Whether you are a fresh graduate seeking your first job, an experienced professional exploring new opportunities, or an organization looking to recruit skilled talent, Rojgar-Nepal provides a comprehensive and efficient solution to make the hiring process more accessible, transparent, and effective.

The project is continuously evolving with the goal of creating a reliable and feature-rich employment platform that serves the needs of job seekers and employers throughout Nepal.
